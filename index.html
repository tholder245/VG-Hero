The racing figher, Captain Falcon

Fighter Bio

Captain Falcon is a powerful rushdown fighter, utilizing his quick speed and high knockback attacks. A player who would be attracted to using Captain Falcon would likely exhibit the following traits: Aggressive, unrelenting, and most importantly, stylish.
